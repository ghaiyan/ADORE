Introduction of ADORE

Automatic detection of Corrosion Levels in Carbon Steel Using Transfer Learning Neural Network

Based on carbon steel corrosion degree data, the ADORE method obtains the model by fine-tuning and training several neural networks. The website integrates several image classification models, including AlexNet, DenseNet, Inception V3, ResNeXt, VGG, and others.
